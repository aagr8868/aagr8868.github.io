Risk of Rain 2 Item Generator








Welcome to the Risk of Rain 2 Item Generator! This tool allows you to randomly generate items for multiple players.

This tool was inteded for the Risk of Rain Shrin of Order Challenge. The rules for the challenge are simple, each player can only use the item generated for them.
Scraps, elemental equipments, and story items will not be generated. A list of items that can be generated can be shown here.

To play, first enable the artifact of command. Then, Simply choose the number of players and the DLCs you want to include, then click "Submit" to see the generated items for each player.
